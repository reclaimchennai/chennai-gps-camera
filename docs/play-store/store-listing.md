## Full description (≤4000 chars)

```
Chennai GPS Camera turns your phone into a location-stamp camera for civic
reporting, field work, and proof-of-place photos — without giving up your
privacy. Every photo and video is stamped with a clean, accurate location
card, and nothing you shoot ever leaves your phone.

WHAT IT STAMPS
• Full address and locality, reverse-geocoded for readable place names
• GPS coordinates (latitude / longitude) with accuracy
• DIGIPIN — India Post's official offline digital address code
• Date and time with your chosen format
• Civic jurisdiction: municipal corporation, ward, zone and police limits,
  resolved from map data built into the app — no internet needed
• Ambient sound level and an inset mini-map

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
• No account, no sign-up, no ads, no tracking, no analytics
• Photos and videos are saved on your device and are never uploaded to us
• Works offline — the jurisdiction and DIGIPIN lookups run entirely on-device
• Address lookup only sends coordinates (never your photos) to a map service,
  and only when you're online

BUILT FOR REAL USE
• Fast, native-feeling capture — shoot photos back to back
• Records video with the location card and audio baked straight into the file
• Live face blur (experimental) to protect bystanders, burned into photos and
  videos
• Powerful photo annotation: draw, arrows, text, highlight, and blur/redact
• Customisable watermark: pick the fields, size, position, theme and opacity
• Add your social handles to the stamp
• Save straight to your gallery

Chennai GPS Camera is a free, open project from the Reclaim Chennai civic-tech
family. Built for Chennai first, with offline jurisdiction data for other
Indian cities too.

Privacy policy: https://cam.reclaimchennai.city/privacy.html
```
